Prior Booking Information

  1. 50% Advance needs to be paid to confirm the booking.
  2. Balance Payment to be paid Upon Arrival.
  3. Guests should produce valid Indian Govt. ID during Check-in ( Aadhar / PAN / Driving license / Voters ID / Passport ) . The Person booking should be above 21 years of age. Declaration form to be signed prior check-in. Child below 5 years Complimentary (need adhar card of child).
  4. Standard Facilities Includes: Indoor & Out Door Games.
  5. Children Aged between 5 to 12 years charged at half price of adults package, below 5 years is complimentary . Kids to be accompanied by an adult all time in the property , as management is not responsible for any damages , losses and accident
  6. MC/PA system & Team Building Activities can be provided on request at additional Cost.
  7. Full refund if cancelled before 7 days of Check-in.
